Forts of Maharashtra

Sindhudurg Fort

Location: Sindhudurg District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1664

Significance: Built to defend the Maratha coastline from foreign invasions. It is one of the most well-preserved sea forts in Maharashtra and holds a significant place in Maratha history.

Murud-Janjira Fort

Location: Raigad District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Siddis of Janjira

Year: 15th Century

Significance: Known for its formidable sea defenses and strategic location, this fort remained unconquered during various invasions.

Vijaydurg Fort

Location: Sindhudurg District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1190

Significance: One of the most strategically important forts, it played a key role in controlling the coastline and trade routes.

Khanderi Fort

Location: Raigad District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1670

Significance: Known for its role in controlling the maritime route and its strategic position near the Khanderi island.

Underi Fort

Location: Raigad District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1670

Significance: Served as a supporting fort to Khanderi and was part of the strategic defense network along the coast.

Kanhoji Angre Fort

Location: Raigad District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Kanhoji Angre

Year: Early 18th Century

Significance: Named after the famous Maratha naval admiral, this fort was crucial in the naval battles against the Portuguese and British.

Vasai Fort

Location: Palghar District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Portuguese

Year: 1534

Significance: Once an important Portuguese stronghold, it was later captured by the Marathas. The fort is known for its impressive Portuguese architecture.

Colaba Fort

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Built By: British East India Company

Year: 17th Century

Significance: Located at the entrance of the Mumbai harbor, it played a crucial role in the defense of Mumbai.

Elephanta Fort

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Hindu Kings

Year: 5th-8th Century

Significance: Famous for its rock-cut temples and sculptures dedicated to Lord Shiva, this fort has a rich cultural and historical heritage.

Sewri Fort

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Portuguese

Year: 1680

Significance: Used as a watchtower, it played a role in monitoring the Mumbai coastline and protecting the harbor area.

Kopineshwar Fort

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Marathas

Year: 17th Century

Significance: A smaller fort with historical significance, it was part of the defense network for the Mumbai region.

Alibaug Fort

Location: Raigad District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Ali Adil Shah

Year: 15th Century

Significance: Known for its historic architecture and strategic location on the Arabian Sea coast.

Mandwa Fort

Location: Raigad District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1680

Significance: Strategically important for controlling the Mandwa region and providing defense against maritime threats.
