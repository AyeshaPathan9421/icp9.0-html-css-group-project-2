About Us

Our mission is to honor Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj by celebrating his leadership, bravery, and commitment to justice. We aim to preserve his legacy, educate others about his life, and inspire unity and courage in future generations. By promoting his values and cultural heritage, we strive to keep his story alive and empower individuals to make a lasting impact.
